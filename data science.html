Data Science

Data Science is an interdisciplinary field that combines statistical methods, computational techniques, and domain knowledge to extract meaningful insights from structured and unstructured data. It involves various processes like collecting, cleaning, analyzing, and interpreting large datasets to uncover patterns, trends, correlations, and make data-driven decisions.Data science is a critical field that powers decision-making, optimization, and predictive modeling across industries like healthcare, finance, marketing, e-commerce, and more.
